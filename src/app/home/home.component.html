Air Compressors, Gas Compressors, Compressor Service, Compressor Parts

TEXAS COMPRESSION SERVICES provides the Industrial, Oil & Gas, Petrochemical and Medical industries with Lubricated & Oil-Free Rotary Screw Compressors, Reciprocating Compressors, Centrifugal Compressors, Gas Compressors, and other Compressed Air Equipment.

Other Products and Services

TEXAS COMPRESSION SERVICES also provides refrigerated air dryers, desiccant air dryers and membrane air dryers to clean and dry compressed air or gas. To accommodate the gas industry, TEXAS COMPRESSION SERVICES offers a complete line of Gardner Denver and LeROI gas compressors.

Custom Solutions

In addition to supplying stand alone compressors, our engineering team is here to help customize systems to meet your specific requirements.
